- notes: **First migration-pattern concept** in the wiki. Captures the additive-parallel-write pattern (legacy keeps writing → new path runs alongside → failures swallowed → observed for a window → ownership flips at cutover) abstracted out of Kivora's Tier 1 Finding-schema migration. Three properties together define the pattern: additive (not replacing), failure-swallowing (in the new path only, removed at cutover), and idempotent (unique-index + find-then-insert).

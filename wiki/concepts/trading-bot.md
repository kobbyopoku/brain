# Trading bot

> A 24/7 API-connected automation that executes a trading strategy at scale, framed by the canonical source not as a shortcut but as the product itself — without which the edge disappears.

## Definition

A trading bot is API-connected automation that executes a trading strategy continuously and at scale. The single ingested source insists the bot is not a convenience layer over a strategy but the thing that makes the edge real: "the bot isn't a shortcut — it's the product. Without it, the edge disappears."

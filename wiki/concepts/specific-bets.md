# Specific Bets

> The unit of real product work in Doshi's framing: concrete bets on concrete features, from which a product's strategic shape is derived rather than chosen in the abstract. Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/shreyas-get-to-the-core-of-the-thing]].

## Definition

Stub. In [[wiki/sources/shreyas-get-to-the-core-of-the-thing]], **specific bets** ("your specific bets on specific features") are the unit of real product work. The product's shape — wide vs deep — is *derived* from the bets, not chosen as an abstract binary. This makes specific bets the lower-altitude object that [[abstraction-altitude-framing|altitude framing]] should descend toward.

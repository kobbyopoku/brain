# Barbell Strategy

> Taleb's risk structure: place the bulk of capital in a maximally conservative mode and a small slice in asymmetric tail bets, leaving the middle empty — "only stability plus antifragility."

## Why it matters

Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/retrochainer-claude-700k-musk-tweets]] as the explicit organizing framework for the source's whole trading strategy.

## Treatment across sources

- [[wiki/sources/retrochainer-claude-700k-musk-tweets]] frames it as Taleb's structure named explicitly: bulk capital in maximally conservative mode (market making) plus a small slice in asymmetric tail bets, with an empty middle — "Only stability plus antifragility." It is the organizing framework for the whole strategy.

## Sub-claims and details

- The conservative leg is implemented as market making; the asymmetric leg as small tail bets ("lottery tickets"). ([[wiki/sources/retrochainer-claude-700k-musk-tweets]])
- The middle of the distribution is deliberately left empty. ([[wiki/sources/retrochainer-claude-700k-musk-tweets]])
- Attributed to Taleb. ([[wiki/sources/retrochainer-claude-700k-musk-tweets]])

# Agentic AI

> Consumer-platform framing for AI that takes actions, completes workflows, makes decisions, and coordinates across apps — a "digital operator" rather than a chatbot. Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/shabnam-google-2026-roadmap-keynote]].

## Definition

Stub. In [[wiki/sources/shabnam-google-2026-roadmap-keynote]], **agentic AI** is named as Google's next major phase: AI that takes actions, completes workflows, makes decisions, and coordinates across apps — framed as "your digital operator," not a chatbot. This is a consumer-platform framing, distinct from the builder-side agent concepts elsewhere in this wiki ([[agentic-loop]], [[multi-agent-orchestration]], [[augmented-llm]]).

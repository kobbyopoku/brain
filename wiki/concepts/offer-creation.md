# Offer creation

> The practice of designing an irresistible commercial offer — and the assets that sell it — rather than building a product and merely describing it.

## Definition

**Offer creation** is the step where a customer's wants are translated into a compelling commercial proposition. In [[wiki/sources/leopardracer-one-person-business-2026-ai]] it is operationalized as a three-prompt chain run in order: (1) build a detailed customer avatar, (2) generate an irresistible first offer from that avatar plus offer-creation principles, (3) draft a compelling landing page from the offer. The AI is "trained" on frameworks from legendary marketers, salesmen, and copywriters before running the chain.

## Why it matters

The source's thesis is that most people build a boring product and merely describe it, leaving money on the table. Treating the offer as a designed artifact — distinct from the product — is what makes a one-person business sellable. The avatar-first chain makes this repeatable with AI.

## Treatment across sources

- [[wiki/sources/leopardracer-one-person-business-2026-ai]] frames it as a three-prompt chain run in order — detailed customer avatar -> irresistible first offer (from avatar + offer-creation principles) -> compelling landing-page draft — built with AI "trained" on frameworks from legendary marketers/salesmen/copywriters; contrasts with most people who build a boring product and merely describe it.

## Sub-claims and details

- The chain runs in a fixed order: avatar, then offer, then landing page. ([[wiki/sources/leopardracer-one-person-business-2026-ai]])
- The offer is generated from the customer avatar plus offer-creation principles. ([[wiki/sources/leopardracer-one-person-business-2026-ai]])
- The landing-page draft is produced downstream from the offer. ([[wiki/sources/leopardracer-one-person-business-2026-ai]])
- The AI is primed with frameworks from legendary marketers, salesmen, and copywriters. ([[wiki/sources/leopardracer-one-person-business-2026-ai]])
- Stated failure mode: building a boring product and merely describing it. ([[wiki/sources/leopardracer-one-person-business-2026-ai]])

## Open questions and contradictions

- The source asserts the offer matters more than the product but does not specify how to validate an offer before building. A source on offer testing would settle this.

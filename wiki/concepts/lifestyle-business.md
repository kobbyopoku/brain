# Lifestyle business

> The one-person business archetype that social media first enabled and AI now accelerates — lower barrier to entry, higher skill cap.

## Definition

A **lifestyle business** here is the one-person (solopreneur) business archetype. Per [[wiki/sources/leopardracer-one-person-business-2026-ai]], social media first made this archetype viable and AI now accelerates it: the barrier to entry is lower while the skill cap is higher. The source frames an aspirational target of $1M/yr — $83,333/mo, $2,777/day — reachable via course, subscription, freelancing, or coaching paths.

## Why it matters

This is the operating context the source's whole playbook serves: customer avatar, offer creation, and content waterfall are all tools for a single operator. For the wiki owner's interest cluster in AI-enabled small-business and solo operations, the lifestyle business is the unit the tooling is built around.

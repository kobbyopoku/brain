# Multimodal AI

> AI that accepts and produces across multiple input and output modalities — text, image, video, voice, sketch, interface — collapsing the distance between an idea and its execution.

## Why it matters

Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/shabnam-google-2026-roadmap-keynote]] as the goal behind a flagship model, framed as reducing idea-to-execution distance to near zero.

## Treatment across sources

- [[wiki/sources/shabnam-google-2026-roadmap-keynote]] frames it as Gemini Omni's "create anything from any input" (text-to-video, image-to-app, voice-to-workflow, sketch-to-interface) — the multimodal-generation goal of reducing the distance from idea to execution to near zero.

## Sub-claims and details

- The named transformation pairs are text-to-video, image-to-app, voice-to-workflow, and sketch-to-interface. ([[wiki/sources/shabnam-google-2026-roadmap-keynote]])

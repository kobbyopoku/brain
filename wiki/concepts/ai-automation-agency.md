# AI automation agency

> The services-business model of automating a painful manual workflow for a "boring" small business and charging for it. Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/entities/everestchris6|everestchris6]]'s thread.

## Definition

This is a **stub**. The AI automation agency is the supply-side delivery vehicle for [[ai-automation-services]]: find a non-technical small business, ask the owner what they hate doing, automate one painful thing (e.g. a voice agent, an invoicing dashboard, a direct-mail system), and charge for it. In [[wiki/sources/everestchris6-100m-ai-opportunity]] it is grounded in three of the author's own builds (a concrete-contractor voice agent, a boat-maker invoicing dashboard, a luxury-golf-cart church-outreach postcard system).

## Treatment across sources

- [[wiki/sources/everestchris6-100m-ai-opportunity]] frames it as "the $100M opportunity" — the demand-side counterpart to the wiki's AI-automation-services supply-side playbooks — and reports concrete delivered builds.

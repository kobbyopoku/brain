# Persona consistency

> The practice of locking one recognizable digital identity — same face, vibe, personality, and voice — across every piece of generated content, named by its canonical source as the hardest part of building an AI influencer.

## Definition

Persona consistency is the practice of keeping a single synthetic persona recognizable — same face, vibe, personality, and voice — across every post. The canonical source calls this its "hardest part" and achieves it via generated reference shots (multiple angles, expressions, and poses), positioning consistency as more important than the underlying generation technology.

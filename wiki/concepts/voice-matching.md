# Voice matching

> Teaching an AI assistant to write in a user's own voice by feeding it samples and an explicit voice profile.

## Definition

Voice matching is described as Layer 4 of a personal AI system: the practice of teaching Claude a user's writing voice. The method uploads 5–10 of the user's best writing samples as Project Knowledge, adds a VOICE PROFILE instruction (short sentences, simple words, action-first openings, no filler), and saves the result as a reusable custom Style.

## Why it matters

A consistent machine-reproducible voice is a content-leverage tool — it lets Godwin's AI assistants draft in his register without per-piece correction. It overlaps directly with the Content OS voice-profile method and with the Helm voice-profiles work the wiki already tracks.

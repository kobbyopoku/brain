# Organic UGC Engine

> A self-made short-form video production system that treats organic videos as perpetual, appreciating assets rather than paid attention that dies when spend stops.

## Definition

The **Organic UGC Engine** is the short-form video production system in [[wiki/sources/mattgittleson-vibecoded-b2c-375k-exit]]: a repeatable factory for organic user-generated-style content. Its mechanics are 50 hook clips, a maximum of 3 uses per demo, batch-filming, a 50/25/25 portfolio split, and a cap of 3 posts per day per account. "Viral sense" is treated as a compounding meta-skill. The core distinction: organic videos are perpetual appreciating assets, whereas paid attention dies the moment spend stops.

## Why it matters

It is the distribution engine that makes [[format-first-product-ideation]] pay off. Because organic reach compounds and does not decay with budget, the engine is the source's account of how a one-person B2C app generated enough demand to reach a fast strategic exit without paid acquisition dependence.

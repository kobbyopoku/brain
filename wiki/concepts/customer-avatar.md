# Customer avatar

> A detailed buyer profile that serves as the foundation every marketing material, offer, and landing page is built on.

## Definition

A **customer avatar** is a detailed profile of the target buyer. In [[wiki/sources/leopardracer-one-person-business-2026-ai]] it is produced by an AI interview prompt, kept on file, and referenced for all marketing materials; it is the first artifact in the offer-creation chain and the foundation on which the offer and landing page are built.

## Why it matters

The avatar is load-bearing: in the source's workflow, both the offer and the landing page are derived from it. A precise avatar makes downstream AI-generated assets specific to a real buyer instead of generic, which is the difference the source draws between an irresistible offer and a boring description.

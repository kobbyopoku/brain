# Customer Insight

> Deep knowledge of what specific customers need — the substrate from which real, differentiating product bets are drawn. Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/shreyas-get-to-the-core-of-the-thing]].

## Definition

Stub. In [[wiki/sources/shreyas-get-to-the-core-of-the-thing]], **customer insight** is the substrate that strategic binaries let teams avoid: real differentiating bets come from deep knowledge of what specific customers need — concretely, "the one whose call you took yesterday." Strategy posed at high [[abstraction-altitude-framing|altitude]] dodges this substrate.

## Treatment across sources

- [[wiki/sources/shreyas-get-to-the-core-of-the-thing]] frames it as the substrate strategic binaries let teams avoid; real differentiating bets come from deep knowledge of what specific customers need ("the one whose call you took yesterday").

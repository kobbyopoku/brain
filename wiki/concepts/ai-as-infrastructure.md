# AI as infrastructure

> The framing of AI not as a product feature but as a foundational utility — comparable to electricity, cloud computing, or the internet — that runs underneath everything.

## Why it matters

Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/shabnam-google-2026-roadmap-keynote]] as the framing behind a vendor strategy of embedding AI across an entire product portfolio rather than shipping it as a discrete app.

## Treatment across sources

- [[wiki/sources/shabnam-google-2026-roadmap-keynote]] frames it as Google treating AI "like electricity / cloud computing / the internet itself" — foundational, not a feature — appearing across Search, Workspace, Android, YouTube, Shopping, Chrome, Cloud, XR, and developer tools.

## Sub-claims and details

- The surfaces named as carrying embedded AI are Search, Workspace, Android, YouTube, Shopping, Chrome, Cloud, XR, and developer tools. ([[wiki/sources/shabnam-google-2026-roadmap-keynote]])

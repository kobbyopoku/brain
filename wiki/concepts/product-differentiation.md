# Product Differentiation

> The hard product question that abstract strategic binaries dodge: whether you can conceive real differentiation against stiff competition and build it creatively and quickly. Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/shreyas-get-to-the-core-of-the-thing]].

Stub.

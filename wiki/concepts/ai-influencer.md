# AI influencer

> A fully synthetic virtual-persona account that posts AI-generated photos, reels, and voice content and is monetized across multiple revenue streams — framed by its canonical source as "an entirely new type of online business."

## Definition

An AI influencer is a fully synthetic virtual persona — not a real person — operated as a social-media account that posts AI-generated photos, reels, and voice content and is monetized across roughly ten revenue streams. The canonical source frames it as "an entirely new type of online business," in which audiences engage based on content and emotion rather than on whether the creator is real.

## Why it matters

The concept sits at the intersection of AI content generation and the creator economy, and the wiki's sources position it as a fast-scaling, low-barrier 2026 online business model. It is a concrete instance of [[online-business-models-2026]] and of the broader move toward AI-creators as a distribution layer for products.

## Open questions and contradictions

- The two sources differ in subject: one centers fully-synthetic personas, the other centers human creators building an AI-niche audience. They share the monetization-via-audience thesis but disagree on whether the creator is real.

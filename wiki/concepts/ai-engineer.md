# AI Engineer (path)

> A solo-AI business path in which a practitioner wires AI models into software products — framed as the highest-skill, highest-pay, and most defensible of the solo-AI business models.

## Definition

The **AI Engineer path** is one of five solo-AI business models catalogued in [[wiki/sources/exploraX_-5-solo-ai-business-models]]. The AI engineer's job is to **wire AI models into software products** — building the integration layer that turns a raw model into a working feature inside an application. The source presents the path as structured by Sajjaad Khader's **5-layer pyramid** ([[wiki/entities/sajjaad-khader|Sajjaad Khader]]).

## Why it matters

Among the five models in the source, the AI Engineer path is positioned as the one with the steepest skill requirement and, correspondingly, the strongest defensibility and pay. It sits at the opposite end of the spectrum from low-barrier paths (e.g. [[selling-websites]]) where the bottleneck is selling rather than building. For Godwin — a software engineer working full-time on AI — this is the path closest to his existing skill base.

## Treatment across sources

- [[wiki/sources/exploraX_-5-solo-ai-business-models]] frames it as **Model 2**: the highest-skill, highest-pay, most defensible path. An AI engineer wires AI models into software products, with the work structured as Sajjaad Khader's 5-layer pyramid.

## Sub-claims and details

- **Defining work**: wiring AI models into software products ([[wiki/sources/exploraX_-5-solo-ai-business-models]]).
- **Structure**: organized as [[wiki/entities/sajjaad-khader|Sajjaad Khader]]'s 5-layer pyramid ([[wiki/sources/exploraX_-5-solo-ai-business-models]]).
- **Money** ([[wiki/sources/exploraX_-5-solo-ai-business-models]]):
  - Salaried: $200-354k+.
  - Freelance: $100-300/hr.
  - Retainers: $5-15k/mo.

# Market making

> Simultaneously quoting both sides of an order book to capture the bid-ask spread, earning from the act of others' trading rather than from predicting market outcomes.

## Why it matters

Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/retrochainer-claude-700k-musk-tweets]] as the source's primary money-maker — an outcome-independent income mechanism — and as the conservative leg of a barbell trading structure.

## Treatment across sources

- [[wiki/sources/retrochainer-claude-700k-musk-tweets]] frames it as the source's primary money-maker: simultaneously quoting both sides of an order book to capture the spread, earning from the act of others' trading rather than from predicting outcomes — "The spread is the edge. Volume is the engine." Income is outcome-independent.

## Sub-claims and details

- The edge is the spread; the engine is volume. ([[wiki/sources/retrochainer-claude-700k-musk-tweets]])
- Income from market making is independent of whether any given trade's underlying prediction is correct. ([[wiki/sources/retrochainer-claude-700k-musk-tweets]])

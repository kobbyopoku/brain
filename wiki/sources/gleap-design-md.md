# Gleap Design System (DESIGN.md from Refero)

> *"Crisp canvas, magenta highlight"* — Gleap's customer-feedback / live-chat tooling design: spacious light layouts, soft rounded containers, single highly-saturated magenta-purple accent, and a four-typeface system pairing PP Editorial New display with Switzer body.

## TL;DR

Gleap's design pairs editorial display typography (PP Editorial New) with functional sans-serif body (Switzer) and Arial/system-ui fallbacks. Single magenta-purple brand accent reserved for primary actions and signaling. Spacious layouts, soft rounded containers — calm yet energetic mood. Single weight (400) at display sizes; PP Editorial New at 48px and 62px is the brand voice.

## Key takeaways

- **Editorial-meets-functional typography**: PP Editorial New (classical, editorial) for display + Switzer (geometric sans) for body — high-contrast typeface pairing.
- **Single magenta-purple accent**: rationed to interactive elements and brand signaling. The "highlight" in the philosophy phrase.
- **Soft rounded containers**: differentiator from Mercury's harder-edged dark-mode dashboards.
- **Four-typeface fallback chain**: PP Editorial New → Switzer → Arial → system-ui — explicit fallback hierarchy from custom to system.
- **Devtools category fit**: Gleap is in the developer-tools category (customer feedback tooling for SaaS) — design balances editorial elegance with functional restraint.

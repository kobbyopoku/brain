# D&B D-U-N-S® Number outreach (Shariq Quraishi)

> An inbound sales/outreach email from [[wiki/entities/dun-and-bradstreet|Dun & Bradstreet]] offering to issue a free [[wiki/entities/d-u-n-s-number|D-U-N-S® Number]] — a global business identifier — and to upsell "value-added services." Filed because establishing global business credibility is directly relevant to [[wiki/entities/godwin-opoku-duah|Godwin]]'s companies ([[wiki/entities/roam-labs|ROAM Labs]], Brolly Africa) as they pursue international clients and partners.

## TL;DR

[[wiki/entities/shariq-quraishi|Shariq Quraishi]] of [[wiki/entities/dun-and-bradstreet|Dun & Bradstreet]] SAMEA offers a free, standard D-U-N-S® Number (a 9-digit global business identifier) with ~30 working days issuance. To proceed, the business must submit its registration/incorporation certificate, a completed company-information template, and a company profile, with name/address exactly matching a valid address proof. The email also flags unspecified paid "value-added services."

## Key takeaways

- The **standard D-U-N-S® Number issuance is free** and quoted at **30 working days**. Paid "value-added services" are mentioned but not detailed — a likely upsell to watch.
- **Required documents**: (1) Company Registration / Incorporation Certificate; (2) completed Company Information Template (attached to the original email — not captured here); (3) Company Profile.
- **Validation hinges on address consistency**: the company name and address in the questionnaire must *exactly* match the supporting documents, with a valid address proof (utility bill — electricity/water/internet — or a notarized rental agreement).
- Address must be in standard format: Building/Complex Name, Street/Road, City, State, Country. Mismatches cause delays.
- **Marketing claims (uncited, treat as vendor-reported)**: D&B database covers "520+ million businesses worldwide"; "90% of Fortune 500 companies rely on it." (per the email — not independently verified.)
- The sender operates out of D&B's **South Asia, Middle East & Africa (SAMEA)** region.

## Notes

This is a cold/outbound sales email, so its framing is promotional; the headline statistics are vendor-reported and should not be cited as fact without independent verification. The substantive, actionable content is the **document checklist** and the **address-consistency requirement**, which are the real gating factors for issuance. The "value-added services" line is the upsell hook — a D-U-N-S Number itself is free to obtain directly from D&B, so any paid tier (e.g., expedited issuance, credit-building products, monitoring) should be evaluated separately on its own merits.

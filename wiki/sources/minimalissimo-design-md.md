# Minimalissimo Design System (DESIGN.md from Refero)

> *"White gallery canvas"* — the most reductive DESIGN.md in the catalog. Seven achromatic colors only, GeistSans 400-weight only, four type-scale steps. Minor Second 1.067 ratio. The discipline of the brand IS the design system.

## TL;DR

Minimalissimo runs the most reductive DESIGN.md in the catalog: seven achromatic neutrals (Inkwell black through Porcelain white), GeistSans 400-weight only, four-step type scale (12px caption → 24px heading), one 4px-base spacing system, two border-radii (4px links/inputs/buttons; 8px cards). 96px section gap is generous. *"Components maintain ghost-like presence, subtly hinting at interaction."*

## Key takeaways

- **Most reductive DESIGN.md** ingested. Seven colors, one font weight, four type sizes.
- **No accent color** — pure achromatic. The catalog's only true grayscale-only system.
- **Single weight typography** (GeistSans 400) is unusual and identity-defining.
- **Minor Second 1.067** — the smallest type ratio in the catalog. Tight, modular hierarchy.
- **96px section gap** despite tight type — preserves the airiness.

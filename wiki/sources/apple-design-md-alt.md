# Apple Alternate Surface Design System (DESIGN.md from Refero)

> *"Precise Canvas, Vivid Product"* — a second Apple DESIGN.md surface in Refero's catalog, distinct from the [[wiki/sources/apple-design-md|MacBook Neo product page]]. Adds Sky Teal `#00a1b3` and Royal Violet `#8668ff` accent colors for product feature highlights, uses a different (smaller) type scale, and includes Arial as a structural fallback.

## TL;DR

The alt Apple surface keeps Apple's identity-defining single-CTA-blue + monochrome-neutrals + carved-typography core, but diverges from MacBook Neo on three axes: it adds **Sky Teal + Royal Violet accents** for feature highlights (MacBook Neo has only Citrus/Indigo/Blush product gradients), uses a **different type scale topping at 44px** (vs MacBook Neo's 80-96px), and explicitly includes **Arial as a structural fallback** for input fields.

## Key takeaways

- **Two Apple DESIGN.md surfaces, not one**: Refero's catalog includes both. They share core but diverge on accents and type scale.
- **Accent palette broader**: this surface adds Sky Teal `#00a1b3` and Royal Violet `#8668ff` for product feature highlights. The MacBook Neo surface has no such accents — only the single Azure CTA blue + product gradients.
- **Smaller type scale**: 8 / 12 / 14 / 17 / 18 / 20 / 24 / 34 / 44 px. Tops at 44px vs MacBook Neo's 80-96px display.
- **Three weights**: 300, 400, 600. Light + regular + semi-bold — same restraint as MacBook Neo.
- **SF Pro Text + SF Pro Display + Arial fallback**: same primary typefaces as MacBook Neo, plus explicit Arial for input fields.
- **Same philosophy phrase tradition**: a stark white presentation surface — but framed differently (*"premium product imagery pop with singular focus"* vs MacBook Neo's *"gallery wall at natural light"*).

## Notable quotes

> "Precise Canvas, Vivid Product. A stark white presentation surface designed to make premium product imagery pop with singular focus."

## Notes

- **Why two Apple DESIGN.md files?** Apple's marketing site is enormous — different product pages have meaningfully different design treatments. MacBook Neo product page (extreme display type, single CTA blue, theatrical product gradients) ≠ general Apple presentation surface (broader accent palette, smaller type scale, more conventional buttons). Refero appears to catalog both as distinct style references.
- **Composition warning for agents**: the two surfaces don't compose without conflict — the alt's Sky Teal + Royal Violet accents would clash with MacBook Neo's product-gradient-only chromatic discipline. An agent generating Apple-voice UI should pick one surface and stay consistent.
- **Refero catalog implication**: a brand may have *multiple* DESIGN.md entries for different product surfaces. The wiki has now ingested 2 Apple surfaces. Future ingests may surface 2nd surfaces for other brands.

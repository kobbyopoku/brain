# Family Design System (DESIGN.md from Refero)

> *"Pixar storyboard on cream paper"* — flat-illustrated character system as primary identity. The most chromatically rich DESIGN.md in the catalog: 4 brand accents + 6 illustration accents + 2 semantic. Custom Family display + Inter body. Cards use **inset stone borders** instead of drop shadows for flat tactility.

## TL;DR

Family's identity is illustration-led: "wobbly blob creatures with stick legs and expressive faces" function as the primary visual language. Color palette is chromatically dense (10 saturated colors total) but governed by strict role rules: Brand Accents (Ember/Meadow/Sky/Sunburst) for hero CTAs and brand moments, Illustration Accents (Deep Amber/Ocean/Ice/Spearmint/Flamingo/Violet Pop) for character fills only. Warm Canvas `#fbfaf9` ground — never pure white. **Inset stone border (`#f2f0ed` 1px shadow)** replaces drop shadows on cards. Custom Family typeface restricted to display sizes 44-68px only; Inter handles everything else.

## Key takeaways

- **Most colors of any DESIGN.md ingested**: 10 saturated values + Coral Red and Valid Green semantic.
- **Chromatic discipline by role**: Brand Accents for CTAs, Illustration Accents for character fills. Strict separation.
- **Illustration-as-identity**: the brand IS the wobbly characters; the rest of the system serves them.
- **Inset stone border** replaces drop shadow — flat tactility instead of depth.
- **Position illustrations asymmetrically, overlapping headline text** — explicit rule. Layout instruction unusual for a DESIGN.md.
- **Warm Canvas `#fbfaf9`** — never pure white as page background.

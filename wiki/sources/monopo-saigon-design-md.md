# monopo saigon Design System (DESIGN.md from Refero)

> *"Shifting gradient depths on darkness"* — black-canvas system with organic gradient hero (green → orange → red), Roobert primary + Raleway display, **225px display type** (largest in the catalog). Buttons at 75.024px radius (decimal preserved).

## TL;DR

monopo saigon is the catalog's most extreme typographic system: Roobert at sizes from 11px to **225px** with a 12-step scale and line-heights from 0.7 to 1.82. Black canvas (`#000000`) + Frost White text. Single Deep Ocean Gradient (green-orange-red) used only in hero. Spacious density: 1078px max-width, 46px section gap, 34px card padding, 14px element gap. Cards 10px radius, buttons 75.024px (a Figma artifact preserved). "Depth and hierarchy through rich, organic background gradients rather than traditional shadows."

## Key takeaways

- **225px display type** is the largest in the catalog — agency-sized statement type.
- **12-step type scale** — most granular among single-typeface brands.
- **Roobert 300/400/600** as primary — agency design tool of choice (Roobert is heavily used in the design industry).
- **Line-height range 0.7-1.82** is very wide; 0.7 is unusually tight (probably display-only).
- **75.024px button radius** — Figma artifact like Base44's `7.42183px` cards.
- **Frost White is `#ffffff`** — used for primary text on dark, contradicting the typical "never pure white as background" rule of other brands. This is dark-mode pure white.

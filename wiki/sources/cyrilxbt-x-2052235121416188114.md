# CyrilXBT — Obsidian + Claude knowledge vault (4-layer architecture, 3K likes)

> Highest-engagement post in the 2026-05-08 batch (3K likes / 90 replies). CyrilXBT describes a four-layer Obsidian-based personal knowledge vault that runs on Claude. **A fresh wild instance of [[llm-wiki-pattern|the LLM Wiki pattern]] with two operational extensions Karpathy doesn't mandate**: (a) an automated **Daily Brief** that surfaces *"3 most interesting connections"* between recent captures and older notes, and (b) a 15-minute **Weekly Synthesis** session examining theses, contradictions, knowledge gaps, and highest-leverage actions. Independent confirmation that the Karpathy pattern propagates with operational improvements when builders run it in production.

## TL;DR

Four layers:
1. **Capture** — Readwise + Airr + Whisper + Telegram bot collect information.
2. **Pipeline** — N8N automation routes content into Obsidian.
3. **Obsidian vault** — permanent storage with five folders (Inbox / Notes / Ideas / Projects / `CLAUDE.md`).
4. **Claude AI** — intelligence layer that finds connections and generates insights.

The thesis: *"Information that goes in but never comes back out is not a knowledge system."* Knowledge tools fail when designed for input only; the *output side* (synthesis, retrieval, surfaced connections) is the part that compounds.

## Key takeaways

### CLAUDE.md as the agent contract — updated weekly

The vault has a top-level `CLAUDE.md` containing context about *who you are*, *current work*, and *expectations for AI assistance*. Updated weekly to keep Claude's understanding current. **Direct match with [[markdown-as-agent-contract]]** at the personal-knowledge layer; the pattern propagates the same way Karpathy described.

### Daily Brief — automated 6am surface-the-connections

A scheduled prompt at 6am identifies *"3 most interesting connections"* between recent captures and older notes, plus patterns and questions worth exploring. **This is structurally [[hot-cache]]'s `_hot.md` mechanism but on a faster cadence (daily) and with explicit *connection-finding* as the agent's job rather than just *recent-state-summary*.** Worth tracking as a new variant of the pattern.

### Weekly Synthesis — 15-minute LLM-driven retrospective

Every week, a 15-minute session examines:
- Emerging theses
- Contradictions in thinking
- Knowledge gaps
- Highest-leverage actions

This is the **lint operation** the Karpathy schema describes, applied at the personal-knowledge level. Same goal: surface contradictions, find what's missing, suggest what to address.

### Six-month compounding claim

CyrilXBT claims that by six months, *"Claude understands your thinking patterns better than you consciously do."* Strong claim, unverified. But aligns with the wiki's existing observation about [[self-annealing]]: persistent agent-aware knowledge improves over time when the directives accumulate fixes.

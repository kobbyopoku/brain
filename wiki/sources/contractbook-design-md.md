# Contractbook Design System (DESIGN.md from Refero)

> *"Playful professionalism, high-contrast clarity"* — contract management platform: bold primary colors (vibrant yellow + strong blue) on a clean spacious background, single typeface (Abcwhyte), visual hierarchy through color blocking + whitespace not elevation.

## TL;DR

Contractbook's design takes a confident playful aesthetic with bold primaries punctuating a clean light background. Single typeface (Abcwhyte) handles everything from headings to body. Vibrant yellow flags primary actions; strong blue carries statement language. The design rejects depth-based hierarchy (no shadows, no elevation tricks) in favor of color blocking + generous whitespace.

## Key takeaways

- **Bold primaries on spacious light**: vibrant yellow (primary actions) + strong blue (statements) instead of accent-on-neutral.
- **Single typeface**: Abcwhyte (humanist sans-serif) for headings, body, UI — friendly + professional.
- **Color blocking over elevation**: hierarchy comes from color and whitespace, not shadows or surfaces. Distinctive choice.
- **Geometric soft shapes**: components and illustrations share a slightly soft geometric vocabulary.
- **Type scale**: 11 / 12 / 14 / 16 / 25 / 28 / 32 / 40 / 48 px. Wide range with editorial display sizes.
- **Two weights**: 400 + 700. Maximum hierarchy contrast through weight + color, minimum through size jumps.

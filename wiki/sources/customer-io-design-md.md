# customer.io Design System (DESIGN.md from Refero)

> *"Architectural Blueprint on Frosted Glass"* — customer.io's lifecycle marketing platform: crisp data-driven interface, achromatic surfaces with cool-tinted dark text, single vivid blue-green for interactive elements, custom saansFont with unusual 475 weight.

## TL;DR

customer.io's design uses clean segmentation and a dominant light theme — achromatic surfaces with cool-tinted dark text. Subtle borders, deliberate lack of heavy shadows, lightweight feel for data-visualization-heavy interfaces. Single typeface (custom saansFont) with precise letter-spacing and a wide weight range including the **unusual 475 weight** (suggests granular custom-cut typography). Wide type scale topping at 96px.

## Key takeaways

- **Custom saansFont**: precise letter-spacing, wide weight range — primary for all content.
- **Unusual 475 weight**: between 400 (regular) and 500 (medium) — suggests a custom-cut variable font with weight granularity beyond standard 100-step increments.
- **Wide type scale**: 12 / 14 / 16 / 18 / 20 / 24 / 30 / 36 / 40 / 48 / 96 px — substantial range. The 96px display is among the largest in the catalog.
- **Cool-tinted text**: dark primary with cool tint (not pure black) — calibrated to feel professional, not stark.
- **Single blue-green accent**: interactive + brand signaling. Distinctive vs the more common "single-blue" or "single-purple" pattern.
- **Lightweight feel**: subtle borders + no heavy shadows = data-viz-friendly chrome.

- **Marketing-automation category fit**: customer.io competes with Mailchimp, HubSpot, Iterable, Braze. The data-viz-light + cool-typographic-restraint combination is well-suited to the category's analytical+operational emphasis.

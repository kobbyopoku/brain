# General Intelligence Company Design System (DESIGN.md from Refero)

> *"Architectural Night Sky"* — bi-modal system: dark illustrative hero + light minimalist UI, bridged by a single Cofounder Blue `#0081c0` accent. PPMondwest custom display + AF body. Five shadow tokens; nine-component pattern set.

## TL;DR

GIC's system blends "evocative, illustrative dark hero with a predominantly minimalist, architectural light UI." Two named typefaces (PPMondwest display 40-54px + AF body 13-18px), a single brand accent (Cofounder Blue `#0081c0`) plus a hover-state Action Azure `#41a1cf`. Twelve-step neutral scale (Pitch Black through Canvas White). Border-radius scaled by component size: 4px buttons → 8px nav → 12-24px cards → 50.496px nav-pill. The "blurred nav item" (background blur + 50.496px radius) is the brand's distinctive signature.

## Key takeaways

- **Twelve-step neutral scale** — granular for a small-brand system.
- **Single accent color** Cofounder Blue with one hover variant. Tight discipline.
- **PPMondwest** — esoteric custom typeface for display.
- **Card radius scales by importance**: 12px (small) → 16px (medium) → 24px (large). Visual hierarchy via radius.
- **Nav pill at 50.496px** — like Base44, decimals preserved from source. Distinctive shape.
- **Nine documented components** — most explicit component list in the catalog.

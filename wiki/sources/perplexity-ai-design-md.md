# Perplexity AI Design System (DESIGN.md from Refero)

> *"Ivory Desk, Graphite Tools"* — pristine brightly-lit workspace; severely limited palette of desaturated off-whites and dark grays; pplxSans single typeface; visual tension between sharp corners and extreme pill-shaped elements.

## TL;DR

Perplexity's design is academic, almost clinical — focused utilitarianism through extreme palette restraint. Single typeface (pplxSans) carries everything from body to navigation to headings. Type scale narrows to just three sizes (12/14/16px) — the smallest scale in the catalog. Only two weights (400, 500). The standout shape choice is the tension between sharp corners (cards, blocks) and extreme pill rounding (interactive chips, buttons).

## Key takeaways

- **Smallest type scale in catalog**: just 12 / 14 / 16 px — three sizes total. Most brands use 6-10 sizes.
- **Two weights only**: 400, 500. Maximum weight discipline.
- **Single typeface**: pplxSans (custom) for body, navigation, headings — cohesive restrained tone.
- **Sharp + pill geometric tension**: cards and content blocks stay sharp; chips and buttons become near-pills. Visual structure-vs-approachability split.
- **Severely limited palette**: desaturated off-whites and dark grays; gray tones do all hierarchy work.
- **AI-tool design conventions**: matches the emerging "AI products are quietly typographic" pattern alongside [[wiki/sources/openai-design-md|OpenAI]] and [[wiki/sources/anthropic-design-md|Anthropic]].

## Notable quotes

> "Ivory Desk, Graphite Tools — a pristine, brightly lit workspace filled with essential gray instruments."

## Notes

- **AI-search category**: Perplexity competes with Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, and Claude on the answer-engine front.
- **Cited in [[wiki/concepts/ai-seo|AI SEO]]**: Perplexity is one of the six citation engines AI SEO targets. So it's both *a brand whose design we have* and *a discovery surface our content should be optimized for*. Reflexive.

# OpenAI Design System (DESIGN.md from Refero)

> *"Blank page before the first word"* — openai.com is a pure-white canvas with near-zero chromatic saturation. OpenAI Sans is the only typographic instrument, carrying display, body, navigation, buttons, inputs. No accent colors, no gradients on chrome, no decorative illustration. Color enters only through editorial imagery.

## TL;DR

OpenAI's site treats white space as the most powerful element. Pure white background. Near-zero chromatic saturation (1%). Black `#000000` and border-gray `#e5e7eb` are the only tools. Custom OpenAI Sans typeface tightly tracked at -0.03em for large display — text feels carved rather than set. Distinctive shape choice: **9999px pills** for chips/inputs alongside a precise **6.08px radius** for cards — extreme roundness paired with one near-flat radius. Color arrives only via editorial imagery (soft-focus flower macros, pastel gradient thumbnails) which feel explosive against the white canvas.

## Key takeaways

- **Editorial restraint as identity**: no chrome color, no decorative illustration. Pure typographic discipline.
- **OpenAI Sans is the entire system**: single typeface for nav, body, headlines, buttons, inputs. -0.03em tracking at 48px display.
- **Two-color core**: black + border-gray. That's it for chrome.
- **Color only via imagery**: editorial photos and thumbnails carry all chromatic punch. The white canvas amplifies them.
- **Distinctive shape system**: 9999px (max pill) for interactive chips/inputs paired with 6.08px (precise near-flat) for cards. Extreme + precise.
- **Carved, not airy display type**: tight tracking at large sizes makes headlines feel chiseled.

## Notable quotes

> "Blank page before the first word — a design that treats white space as the most powerful element, reserving all color for user-generated and editorial content."

## Notes

- **Cross-cite with [[wiki/entities/anthropic|Anthropic]]'s DESIGN.md**: both companies are in the catalog, both lean typographic and restrained — but Anthropic uses warm cream and word-level underlines (research-journal aesthetic) while OpenAI uses pure white and near-pill+precise-near-flat (blank-page aesthetic). The two brands' typographic restraint reads as opposing chromatic philosophies.
- **AI-products design pattern**: minimalist white + custom typeface seems to be the emerging convention — OpenAI, [[wiki/sources/perplexity-ai-design-md|Perplexity]], and [[wiki/sources/anthropic-design-md|Anthropic]] all converge on it. *AI-product brand restraint* may be a category-level move worth tracking as more AI brands ship DESIGN.md files.

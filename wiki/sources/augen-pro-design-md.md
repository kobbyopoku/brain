# Augen Pro Design System (DESIGN.md from Refero)

> *"Architectural Blueprint on White Marble"* — third Refero brand to use this exact philosophy phrase (after [[wiki/sources/stripe-design-md|Stripe]] and [[wiki/sources/column-design-md|Column]]). High-contrast monochrome (near-black on near-white) with a single vivid blue accent. Single typeface PP Neue Montreal at unusually-light weights (350 + 400).

## TL;DR

Augen Pro's design projects *understated precision and future-forward serenity*. Monochrome near-black-on-near-white with single vivid blue accent reserved for interactive elements. Single typeface PP Neue Montreal carries everything — display, body, UI, buttons. The 350 weight is unusually light, appearing in only a handful of catalog brands. Type scale tops out at 27px — tight, restrained.

## Key takeaways

- **Third "blueprint on marble" brand**: same exact philosophy phrase as Stripe and Column — Refero's catalog has this phrase as a distinct visual archetype now triple-cited.
- **Single typeface**: PP Neue Montreal — display + body + UI + buttons + links.
- **Unusually light weights**: 350, 400. The 350 weight is rare in the catalog (most brands center on 400-500-600-700).
- **Single vivid blue accent**: rationed to interactive elements — guides user focus with subtle distinction.
- **Tight type scale**: 10 / 12 / 13 / 14 / 16 / 18 / 27 px — top size is 27px, smaller than most catalog brands.

## Notable quotes

> "Architectural Blueprint on White Marble. Every element is immaculately placed against a pristine, bright background, creating a sense of technical elegance."

## Notes

- **Brand identity uncertain**: *Augen* is German for "eyes" — likely eyewear, eye-care, or vision-tech, but unconfirmed by primary source. Worth a `/brain-ingest` of an Augen Pro homepage clipping when convenient.
- **Three "blueprint" brands** ([[wiki/entities/stripe|Stripe]], [[wiki/entities/column|Column]], Augen Pro) demonstrate Refero's catalog organizes by philosophy phrases as well as by brand — the same phrase can mean *three different executions*: violet/single-typeface/light-weight (Stripe), plum-orange/four-typeface/mid-weight (Column), blue/single-typeface/ultra-light-weight (Augen Pro).

# Titan Design System (DESIGN.md from Refero)

> *"Monochrome financial ledger"* — high-contrast, monochrome aesthetic with one Highlight Orange `#ff9900` accent. Geist + Geist Mono typefaces. **160px button radius and 140px nav radius** — the most extreme pill convention in the catalog. No box-shadows.

## TL;DR

Titan is high-contrast monochrome with a single Highlight Orange accent. Six neutrals from Action Black `#000000` through Canvas White `#ffffff`. Geist for primary type (400/500/700) + Geist Mono for code/data. Critical signature: **buttons at 160px radius, navigation at 140px** — these are the largest pill values in the entire catalog (most brands cap at 9999px or 30-32px). No box-shadows; "use background/border differentiation instead." Nine-step type scale 10-60px with `ss02, ss03` OpenType features.

## Key takeaways

- **160px button radius** is the largest specific (non-9999px) value in the catalog.
- **Single accent color** — Highlight Orange. No other saturated colors permitted.
- **Geist + Geist Mono** — same pairing as Minimalissimo (Geist) and overlaps with Linear's mono aesthetic.
- **OpenType `ss02, ss03`** stylistic-set features for Geist — most brands use defaults.
- **No box-shadows** — same rule as Apple, Anthropic, Mercury.
- **Off-White Sage `#f3efeb`** is the secondary surface — slightly green-tinted vs typical neutral grays.

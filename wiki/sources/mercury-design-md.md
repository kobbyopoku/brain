# Mercury Design System (DESIGN.md from Refero)

> *"Mountain Top Command Center"* — twilight dark palette with single Mercury Blue `#5266eb` brand accent. arcadiaDisplay 360-weight headlines for cinematic clarity. Cards at **0px radius**, buttons at 32-40px pill. **No shadows** — color/opacity shifts only.

## TL;DR

Mercury runs an immersive dark palette (`#171721` Deep Space outer / `#1e1e2a` Midnight Slate primary) with Starlight `#ededf3` as glowing off-white text. Mercury Blue `#5266eb` reserved for primary CTAs only; Ghost Blue `#cdddff` for secondary buttons. arcadiaDisplay 360-weight (light) for major headlines — "twilight" framing emphasized by light-weight on dark canvas. Custom Arcadia typefaces. Base 4px, spacious density (80-120px section gaps), buttons at 32-40px radius pill, **cards at 0px radius** as deliberate signature. Explicit "no shadows; use color and opacity shifts."

## Key takeaways

- **Cards 0px radius** is the signature — almost everywhere else cards are 6-30px.
- **arcadiaDisplay weight 360** is non-standard; suggests a variable axis where 360 is intentional, not a standard 100/200/300/400 scale step.
- **No shadows** — entire system is color-and-opacity-driven.
- **Generous vertical spacing**: 80-120px section gaps. Cinematic.
- **Twilight philosophy**: light typography on near-black canvas. Different from Linear's "command center" by being lighter and more spacious.

## Notable quotes

> "Command center at twilight, expansive and focused."

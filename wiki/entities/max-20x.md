# Max 20x

> Anthropic's $200/month Claude plan tier that carries a weekly Claude Design quota. Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/nateherk-claude-design-tally-brand]].

## Key facts

- **Price**: $200/month (cited to [[wiki/sources/nateherk-claude-design-tally-brand]]).
- **Design quota**: Carries a weekly Claude Design quota that is separate from regular Claude and Claude Code usage (cited to [[wiki/sources/nateherk-claude-design-tally-brand]]).
- **Quota burn (reported)**: nateherk burned 30% of his weekly quota on just a design system plus a pitch deck (cited to [[wiki/sources/nateherk-claude-design-tally-brand]]).

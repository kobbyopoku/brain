# nurijanian

> Builder and writer on X (@nurijanian) who designs agent-ready product-management tooling; author of "/goal for Product Managers".

## Profile

nurijanian is the author of "/goal for Product Managers" ([[wiki/sources/nurijanian-goal-for-product-managers]]) and the builder of [[wiki/entities/pm-os|PM OS]], a product operating layer for product managers. In the post he reframes the `/goal` command not as new model autonomy but as the Ralph Wiggum loop with product design built around it, arguing the leverage comes from sharper product requirements rather than smarter agents. He works on encoding the tacit judgment of PMs into reusable, agent-ready workflows.

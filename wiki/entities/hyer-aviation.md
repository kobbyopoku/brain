# Hyer Aviation

> Private aviation booking service. Cataloged in Refero with a *"monochromatic luxury, sharp contrast"* light-mode philosophy — deep near-black elements punctuating crisp white surfaces, anchored by exceptionally large display typography.

## Profile

This page is a **stub**. Hyer Aviation appears in the wiki only as a Refero-catalog DESIGN.md ingest. No primary source about the company, its operations, or its market is yet ingested.

## Design system summary

- **Theme**: light — crisp white surfaces juxtaposed with deep, almost-black elements; subtle warm accent.
- **Philosophy**: *"commanding, high-contrast aesthetic … sophistication through assertive simplicity."* Imagery takes center stage.
- **Typeface**: **HelveticaNowDisplay** (single typeface for all primary text — headlines, body, buttons, navigation).
- **Type sizes**: extreme range — 17 / 18 / 20 / 23 / 30 / 37 / 52 / 60 / 63 / 131 / 187 px. The 131px and 187px sizes are among the largest in the entire 32-brand catalog.
- **Weights**: 400, 700.
- **Shape**: rounded pills for restraint; ample whitespace.

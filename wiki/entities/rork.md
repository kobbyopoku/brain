# Rork

> AI mobile-app builder platform. Its self-published guide ([[wiki/sources/rork-scale-mobile-app-50k]]) underpins the "building is easy, distribution is hard" premise: the tool makes shipping apps trivial, so the guide focuses on getting downloads.

## Profile

Rork is an AI app-builder platform that lets builders "build whatever app you want in just a couple of prompts." It positions building as nearly solved — "We made the build easy but distribution is still hard" — and claims an app should take no more than two weeks from idea to launch using Rork. The company says its builders "are shipping more apps to the App Store than ever before," and that some builders are "making $20k–$50k a month from apps they built in days or weeks." All facts here derive from Rork's own promotional guide, published from the X account @rork.

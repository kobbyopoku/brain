# D.E. Shaw

> Quantitative fund featured in the catalog of open-source repos from hedge funds and quant firms.

## Profile

D.E. Shaw is a quantitative fund with approximately $90B AUM (source-reported). Jeff Bezos worked there before leaving to start Amazon. The firm funded the development of foundational scientific-Python tooling — IPython, Jupyter, and NumPy — and contributes four catalogued open-source repositories: pyflyby, pjrmi, versioned-hdf5, and nbstripout-fast, per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].

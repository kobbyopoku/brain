# Content Rewards

> A per-view content-clipping / UGC reward-campaign marketplace; the "earning" half of multiple clipping-business playbooks in this wiki.

Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/8xgrowth-100-days-to-10k-clipping]] and [[wiki/sources/VadimStrizheus-hermes-10k-clipping]].

## Key facts

- **X handle**: @contentrewards (cited in [[wiki/sources/8xgrowth-100-days-to-10k-clipping]]).
- **Operator**: Associated with [[wiki/entities/daniel-bitton]], described elsewhere in the wiki as operator of Content Rewards (per [[wiki/sources/VadimStrizheus-hermes-10k-clipping]]).
- **Role in 8xgrowth's plan**: Described as the "earning part" tool (cited in [[wiki/sources/8xgrowth-100-days-to-10k-clipping]]).
- **Mechanic**: Creators pick campaigns/niches to clip; payouts implied to scale with views/account volume (cited in [[wiki/sources/8xgrowth-100-days-to-10k-clipping]]).
- **Market scale**: The clipping economy paid ~$1M/week to clippers in Dec 2025, attributed to Content Rewards (per [[wiki/sources/VadimStrizheus-hermes-10k-clipping]]).

## Positions and claims

- In 8xgrowth's 100-day plan, used by launching 2-3 fresh accounts plus a warming process on Day 1, scaling to 5 more accounts in Month 2 — described in [[wiki/sources/8xgrowth-100-days-to-10k-clipping]].

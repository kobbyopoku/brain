# Opus 4.7

> The Anthropic Claude model that [[wiki/entities/claude-design|Claude Design]] runs on, cited as having the strongest vision in Anthropic's lineup.

## Profile

Opus 4.7 is the Anthropic Claude model powering [[wiki/entities/claude-design|Claude Design]]. Per [[wiki/sources/nateherk-claude-design-tally-brand]] it was released April 16, 2026 — one day before Claude Design — and is described as having the strongest vision in Anthropic's lineup. In Claude Design workflows it is recommended for planning prompts, while the cheaper [[wiki/entities/sonnet-4-6|Sonnet 4.6]] is recommended for in-canvas tweaks. Design "eats tokens hard," especially on Opus 4.7.

# D-U-N-S® Number

> A unique 9-digit identifier assigned by [[wiki/entities/dun-and-bradstreet|Dun & Bradstreet]] to an individual business entity, used to establish global business identity and credibility for verification and due diligence.

## Profile

The D-U-N-S® Number (Data Universal Numbering System) is Dun & Bradstreet's unique 9-digit business identifier. One number is assigned per legal business entity, and it is used by financial institutions, multinational corporations, and partners to verify and run due diligence on a business globally. Standard issuance is offered free of charge. It appears in this wiki because obtaining one is a practical step toward global business credibility for [[wiki/entities/godwin-opoku-duah|Godwin]]'s companies.

## Key facts

- **Format**: a unique **9-digit** identifier, one per business entity. (per [[wiki/sources/dnb-duns-number-outreach]])
- **Issuer**: [[wiki/entities/dun-and-bradstreet|Dun & Bradstreet]]. (per [[wiki/sources/dnb-duns-number-outreach]])
- **Purpose**: establishes global business identity/credibility; used for verification and due diligence by financial institutions, multinationals, and partners. (per [[wiki/sources/dnb-duns-number-outreach]])
- **Cost & timeline**: standard issuance is **free**, quoted at **~30 working days**. (per [[wiki/sources/dnb-duns-number-outreach]])
- **To obtain (per the outreach)**: submit company registration/incorporation certificate, a completed company-information template, and a company profile; company name + address must exactly match a valid address proof in standard format (Building/Complex, Street, City, State, Country). (per [[wiki/sources/dnb-duns-number-outreach]])

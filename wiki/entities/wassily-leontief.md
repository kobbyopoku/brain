# Wassily Leontief

> The Nobel laureate economist, cited in this wiki as a failed prophet of human labor obsolescence.

Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]].

## Key facts

- Nobel laureate economist (cited to [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]]).
- Wrote in 1982 that humans would "go the way of the horse: first diminished and then eliminated" (cited to [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]]).
- Cited by the essay as a prediction that "clearly did not happen" (cited to [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]]).

# Gleap

> Customer feedback / bug reporting / live chat tooling for SaaS products. Cataloged in Refero with a *"crisp canvas, magenta highlight"* light-mode philosophy — high-contrast modern sans-serifs with a single saturated magenta-purple accent.

## Profile

This page is a **stub**. Gleap appears in the wiki only as a Refero-catalog DESIGN.md ingest. No primary source about the company, its product surface, or its market position is yet ingested.

## Design system summary

- **Theme**: light — spacious layouts, soft rounded containers, single highly-saturated magenta-purple accent for primary actions.
- **Philosophy**: *"calm yet energetic feel"* — editorial display fonts shifting to compact functional system fonts for body.
- **Typefaces**: **PP Editorial New** (display headlines — classical/editorial contrast), **Switzer** (body, labels, UI), **Arial** (system fallback for compact data displays), **system-ui** (smallest functional labels).
- **Type sizes** (display): 48 / 62 px.
- **Weight**: 400 (single weight at display).
- **Shape**: soft rounded containers throughout.
- **Brand signature**: magenta-purple accent reserved for interactive guidance.

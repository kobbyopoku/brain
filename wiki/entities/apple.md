## Design system summary

From [[wiki/sources/apple-design-md]]: One Azure `#0071e3` for the Buy CTA, one Cobalt `#0066cc` for inline links — no other non-neutral fills. SF Pro Display + SF Pro Text. **28px card radius is the page's geometric signature.** Letter-spacing scales aggressively with size (-2.11px at 96px display). Zero box-shadows.

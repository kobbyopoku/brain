# Warp

> Developer-tools company (also the name of its block-based terminal product) that built the self-improving community-engagement agent [[wiki/entities/buzz|Buzz]]. Cataloged in [[wiki/entities/open-design|Open Design]] under **Developer Tools**.

## Profile

Warp is a developer-tools company whose name also identifies its modern, block-based terminal product. Its Developer Experience team responds to people mentioning Warp across Twitter, Reddit, and other channels — a community that generates more than a thousand mentions per week. To handle that volume, Warp built an internal agent named [[wiki/entities/buzz|Buzz]] that triages mentions and drafts replies, running on Warp's agent-management product [[wiki/entities/oz|Oz]]. Warp's design surface is cataloged in [[wiki/entities/open-design|Open Design]] under Developer Tools.

# Intel

> Semiconductor company; appears in this wiki as a hardware target for local-AI inference engines. See [[wiki/sources/theahmadosman-inference-engines-local-ai-hardware]].

## Key facts

- **OpenVINO target hardware**: Intel Xeon CPUs, Arc GPUs, Core Ultra, and NPUs are the OpenVINO target hardware, cited in [[wiki/sources/theahmadosman-inference-engines-local-ai-hardware]].
- **SGLang backend**: Intel Xeon is a supported backend in [[wiki/entities/sglang|SGLang]], cited in [[wiki/sources/theahmadosman-inference-engines-local-ai-hardware]].

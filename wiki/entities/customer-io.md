# customer.io

> Marketing automation / lifecycle messaging platform — email, SMS, push, in-app messaging based on customer event data. Cataloged in Refero with an *"Architectural Blueprint on Frosted Glass"* light-mode philosophy — crisp data-driven interface, achromatic surfaces, single vivid blue-green for interactive elements.

## Design system summary

- **Theme**: light — clean segmentation; subtle borders; deliberate lack of heavy shadows; data visualizations stand out.
- **Philosophy**: *"crisp, data-driven interface"* — cool-tinted dark primary text; vivid blue-green accent for interactive + brand signaling.
- **Typeface**: **saansFont** (custom — primary for all content; precise letter-spacing; wide weight range for clear hierarchy).
- **Type sizes**: 12 / 14 / 16 / 18 / 20 / 24 / 30 / 36 / 40 / 48 / 96 px. Very wide scale — 96px display is among the largest.
- **Weights**: **475**, 500, 600, 700. The **475 weight is unusual** — suggests a custom-cut font with weight granularity beyond the standard 100-step increments.

# YouTube Shorts

> YouTube's short-form vertical-video surface, appearing in this wiki as a cross-posting distribution channel and as a scrape target for viral-structure analysis.

Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/rork-scale-mobile-app-50k]] and [[wiki/sources/8xgrowth-100-days-to-10k-clipping]].

## Key facts

- Listed as one of three platforms (with TikTok and Instagram) to cross-post 7 daily posts across, totaling ~21 posts/day. *(per [[wiki/sources/rork-scale-mobile-app-50k]])*
- 8xgrowth's Blueprint Agent scrapes the top 10 viral YouTube Shorts in a chosen niche. *(per [[wiki/sources/8xgrowth-100-days-to-10k-clipping]])*
- The agent "decodes the structure" of the Shorts rather than just watching them, outputting a complete blueprint. *(per [[wiki/sources/8xgrowth-100-days-to-10k-clipping]])*

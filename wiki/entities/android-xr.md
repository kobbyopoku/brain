# Android XR

> Google's XR / AI smart-glasses platform, read in the source as a long-term "screens are temporary" bet.

## Profile

Android XR is Google's extended-reality platform, presented at I/O 2026 as a pervasive theme spanning AI smart glasses, contextual assistants, real-world AI overlays, live translation, and spatial computing. In the wiki it appears as evidence of a long-term bet that ambient, always-observing devices displace screen-bound input. Details below are drawn from the cited press in the source thread.

## Key facts

- **Vendor**: Google.
- Appeared "everywhere" at Google I/O 2026, per the cited The Verge coverage in [[wiki/sources/shabnam-google-2026-roadmap-keynote]].
- Showcased AI smart glasses, contextual assistants, real-world AI overlays, live translation, and spatial computing integrations.

## Positions and claims

- Rationale given: XR continuously observes context (vision, audio, location) where phones require explicit input — argued in [[wiki/sources/shabnam-google-2026-roadmap-keynote]].

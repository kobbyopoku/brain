# Interact Labs

> AI startup (interactlabs.ai) building a conversational, self-assembling replacement for the static website. Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/suyashkarn2-ai-trillion-dollar-blind-spot-static-website]].

This page is a **stub**. Interact Labs appears in the wiki via [[wiki/sources/suyashkarn2-ai-trillion-dollar-blind-spot-static-website]], the company's own thesis-as-pitch authored by [[wiki/entities/suyashkarn2|SuyashKarn2]].

## Key facts

- **Website**: interactlabs.ai (cited in [[wiki/sources/suyashkarn2-ai-trillion-dollar-blind-spot-static-website]]).
- **Pitch**: replace the static website with an interface that converses, adapts, and assembles itself around each visitor in real time, grounded in the company's actual data (cited in [[wiki/sources/suyashkarn2-ai-trillion-dollar-blind-spot-static-website]]).
- **Product capability**: surfaces user intent, objections, priorities, and buying signals as a real-time view for product and sales teams (cited in [[wiki/sources/suyashkarn2-ai-trillion-dollar-blind-spot-static-website]]).
- **Entry point**: an "Interact Now" action; described as best experienced on a desktop or laptop (cited in [[wiki/sources/suyashkarn2-ai-trillion-dollar-blind-spot-static-website]]).
- **Target market**: "the interface layer" between companies and customers — onboarding, documentation, support, sales enablement, in-product education (cited in [[wiki/sources/suyashkarn2-ai-trillion-dollar-blind-spot-static-website]]).

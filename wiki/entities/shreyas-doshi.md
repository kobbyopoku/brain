# Shreyas Doshi

> Product-strategy advisor and X essayist who advises founders and startups, and author of the essay "Get to the Core of the Thing".

## Profile

Shreyas Doshi is a product-strategy advisor who works with founders and startups, posting product-strategy essays on X under the handle `@shreyas`. In the wiki he appears as the author of "Get to the Core of the Thing" (an X long-post, 2026-05-16), in which his advice to a founder running an AI startup — answering the question "go wide or deep?" — frames the entire essay. His central argument there is that high-altitude strategic binaries are theater, and the real product question is always "one level down".

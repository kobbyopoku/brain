# Postiz

> Open-source self-hostable social-media scheduler. Repo: [`gitroomhq/postiz-app`](https://github.com/gitroomhq/postiz-app), built by `@wickedguro`. Schedules across **X, LinkedIn, Instagram, Threads, TikTok, YouTube, Bluesky, Reddit, and more** — single queue across multiple channels. Surfaced in the wiki via [[wiki/sources/shannholmberg-content-os|Shann's Content OS]] as the *publish layer* under his AI-augmented content workflow.

## Profile

This page is a **stub**. Postiz is named in Shann's Content OS thread as the only social-publish tool he recommends; the wiki has no other source treatment yet.

## Key facts

- **Repo**: `gitroomhq/postiz-app`.
- **Maintainer**: @wickedguro.
- **Pricing**: open-source + self-hostable; SaaS at postiz.com.
- **Channels supported**: X, LinkedIn, Instagram, Threads, TikTok, YouTube, Bluesky, Reddit, more.
- **Role**: publish layer that lets agents queue and schedule approved drafts across channels.

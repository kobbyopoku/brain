# EU AI Act

> European Union regulation governing artificial intelligence systems. Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/awrigh01-technical-stack-autonomous-agents]] as the regulatory driver that splits compliance out as its own vendor layer in the autonomous-agent technical stack.

## Key facts

- **High-risk obligations effective date**: August 2, 2026 (per [[wiki/sources/awrigh01-technical-stack-autonomous-agents]]).
- **Article 99 penalties**: up to €35 million or 7% of global turnover (per [[wiki/sources/awrigh01-technical-stack-autonomous-agents]]).
- **Articles 12 and 19**: require automatic event logging allowing regulators to reconstruct system behavior after the fact (per [[wiki/sources/awrigh01-technical-stack-autonomous-agents]]).
- **Targeted by ComplyEdge**: the compliance vendor [[wiki/entities/complyedge|ComplyEdge]] targets enforcement of Articles 9, 12, 13, and 14 (per [[wiki/sources/awrigh01-technical-stack-autonomous-agents]]).

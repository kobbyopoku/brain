# Matt Gittleson

> Non-technical solo founder who built and exited the B2C web app [[wiki/entities/citesure|CiteSure]] for $375,000 in roughly six months, then joined [[wiki/entities/jenni-ai|Jenni AI]].

## Profile

Matt Gittleson is a non-technical solo founder who, by his own account, "can't code." A year out of university and working a 9-to-9 consulting job, he built [[wiki/entities/citesure|CiteSure]] nights-and-weekends and sold it for $375,000 roughly six months after starting. He marketed the product almost entirely through organic short-form video he produced himself, generating 30M views for under $100 in total spend despite having never posted a TikTok before. After the exit he moved to Korea and joined [[wiki/entities/jenni-ai|Jenni AI]], the company that acquired CiteSure.

- **Technical background**: non-technical; states "I can't code" (per [[wiki/sources/mattgittleson-vibecoded-b2c-375k-exit]]).

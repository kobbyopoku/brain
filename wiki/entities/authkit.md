# AuthKit

> Hosted authentication UI by [WorkOS](https://workos.com). Cataloged in Refero with a *"Midnight Command Center"* dark-mode philosophy — frosted-glass surfaces over a deep blue-black canvas, punctuated by restrained muted blues. Imported into the brain via the 2026-05-04 design-md batch.

## Design system summary

- **Theme**: dark — `Midnight Abyss` deep blue-black background, crisp white text, muted achromatic blues for accents.
- **Philosophy**: *"high-tech dashboard glowing softly in a dark room, with frosted glass elements reflecting subtle light."* Internal-dashboard feel; polished and functional without sterile.
- **Typefaces**: **Untitled Sans** (body, forms, buttons, secondary), **aeonikPro** (display headings, brand voice — slightly wider than Untitled Sans for visual contrast), **dotDigital** (small informational text with `tnum` for monospaced numbers).
- **Type sizes**: 12 / 14 / 16 / 18 / 24 px scale. Weights 400/500/600/700.
- **Shape mix**: pill (round) for some interactive elements, subtly rounded for others — deliberate tactile contrast.
- **Mood**: digital precision; restrained palette; subtle inner shadows.

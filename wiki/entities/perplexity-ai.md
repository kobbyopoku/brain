# Perplexity AI

> AI-powered answer engine / search interface that cites its sources. Cataloged in Refero with an *"Ivory Desk, Graphite Tools"* light-mode philosophy — pristine brightly-lit workspace; severely limited palette of desaturated off-whites and dark grays.

## Design system summary

- **Theme**: light — atmosphere of *focused utilitarianism*; severely limited palette.
- **Philosophy**: *"academic, almost clinical aesthetic"* — meticulous use of varying gray tones for hierarchy; sharp corners mixed with extreme pill-shaped elements.
- **Typeface**: **pplxSans** (single custom typeface — body, navigation, headings; cohesive restrained tone).
- **Type sizes**: 12 / 14 / 16 px. Notably narrow scale — only three sizes, smallest catalog.
- **Weights**: 400, 500.
- **Shape signature**: visual tension between sharp corners (cards/blocks) and extreme pill rounding (interactive chips/buttons).

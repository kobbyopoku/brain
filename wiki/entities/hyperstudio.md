## Design system summary

From [[wiki/sources/hyperstudio-design-md]]: Most spartan system in the catalog. Eight neutrals + two accents (Amber Glow `#E7C59A` interactive; Neon Green `#00AC5C` status). Extreme dark ground `#101010`. Three border-radii total. No shadow vocabulary. Aeonik + Input typeface pairing.

# Adaptive

> AI agent-builder platform; the "automation and heavy lifting" half of 8xgrowth's content-clipping playbook.

Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/8xgrowth-100-days-to-10k-clipping]].

## Key facts

- **X handle**: @adaptiveai (cited in [[wiki/sources/8xgrowth-100-days-to-10k-clipping]]).
- **Role in 8xgrowth's plan**: Described as the tool for "automation and heavy lifting" (cited in [[wiki/sources/8xgrowth-100-days-to-10k-clipping]]).

## Positions and claims

In 8xgrowth's 100-day plan, Adaptive is used to build a set of agents (all described in [[wiki/sources/8xgrowth-100-days-to-10k-clipping]]):

- A **Blueprint Agent** that scrapes the top 10 viral YouTube Shorts in a niche and decodes their structure.
- A **Strategy Extractor** agent that reverse-engineers the fastest-growing channels' playbooks.
- An **outlier-analysis** agent comparing viral hits against flops.
- An **Auto-Post Agent** that pulls edited videos from Google Drive and pushes them to socials.

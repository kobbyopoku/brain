# IgnytLabs

> Prajwal Tomar's AI-products agency; the production context where the 4-step Claude Design workflow is applied.

## Profile

IgnytLabs (X: `@ignytlabs`) is the AI-products agency operated by [[wiki/entities/prajwal-tomar|Prajwal Tomar]]. It self-describes as building AI-powered products for startups and businesses. It builds a design system for every client before touching a single page and maintains templates for every deliverable. The agency applies the 4-step [[wiki/entities/claude-design|Claude Design]] workflow for rapid client concepting.

## Key facts

- **X handle**: `@ignytlabs` (per [[wiki/sources/prajwaltomar-claude-design-workflow]]).
- **Self-description**: builds AI-powered products for startups and businesses (per the design-system prompt the author used) (per [[wiki/sources/prajwaltomar-claude-design-workflow]]).
- **Process**: builds a design system for every client before touching a single page (per [[wiki/sources/prajwaltomar-claude-design-workflow]]).
- **Templates**: maintains templates for every deliverable it produces — landing pages, case studies, client proposals, onboarding guides (per [[wiki/sources/prajwaltomar-claude-design-workflow]]).
- **Throughput claim**: uses the 4-step Claude Design workflow for rapid client concepting, reportedly delivering high-fidelity concepts in under an hour and reducing two-week concept timelines to a day (per [[wiki/sources/prajwaltomar-claude-design-workflow]]).
- **Design-system prompt**: used a brand personality of "bold, clean, technical" with deep navy blue primary, electric blue accent, modern/professional fonts, sharp buttons, dark-first mode (per [[wiki/sources/prajwaltomar-claude-design-workflow]]).

# Palantir

> Enterprise software company credited as the origin of the forward-deployed engineer (FDE) role and its on-site-deployment philosophy. Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/vasuman-forward-deployed-engineering-101]].

## Key facts

- **FDE origin**: The term FDE originated at Palantir (cited to [[wiki/sources/vasuman-forward-deployed-engineering-101]]).
- **On-site deployment**: Cited as taking on-site deployment very seriously (cited to [[wiki/sources/vasuman-forward-deployed-engineering-101]]).
- **Afghanistan example (reported)**: In 2010, Palantir worked with a Special Forces group in Afghanistan; FDEs shipped code overnight based on daytime mission feedback (cited to [[wiki/sources/vasuman-forward-deployed-engineering-101]]).

## Positions and claims

- Palantir's CTO is quoted: "you cannot build products for an environment without actually being in the environment itself." (cited to [[wiki/sources/vasuman-forward-deployed-engineering-101]]).

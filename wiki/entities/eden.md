# Eden

> Web-based canvas/workspace app (app.eden.so) hosting duplicatable templates and interview-style AI prompts for building a one-person business.

## Profile

Eden is a web app at app.eden.so / beta.eden.so providing a canvas/workspace with sticky-note instructions and duplicatable templates. It enters this wiki via [[wiki/sources/leopardracer-one-person-business-2026-ai]], which recommends working the brand/content/offer build through Eden on desktop. Eden lets users paste direct social links and point AI at a reference folder, and hosts interview-style prompts that generate a customer avatar, an offer blueprint, and landing-page copy. The duplicatable [[wiki/entities/dan-koe|Dan Koe]] template referenced in the source is hosted on the platform under "dan-s-workspace."

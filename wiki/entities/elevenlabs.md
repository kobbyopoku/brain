## Design system summary

From [[wiki/sources/elevenlabs-design-md]]: Most chromatically restrained system in the catalog. 8 grayscale neutrals + only 2 saturated colors (Ember `#ff4704` + Signal Blue `#0447ff`) used **only for product avatar dots**. Eggshell `#fdfcfc` page ground (never pure white). Hairline 1px shadows. Inputs at 0px radius as editorial distinction. Waldenburg display 300-only + WaldenburgFH 700-only at 14px.

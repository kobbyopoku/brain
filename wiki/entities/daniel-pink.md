# Daniel Pink

> The author, cited in this wiki for predicting mass solopreneurship but mis-timing it.

Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]].

## Key facts

- Wrote [[wiki/entities/free-agent-nation|Free Agent Nation]] in 2001 (cited to [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]]).
- Predicted that within twenty-five years the W-2 lifetime job would be a historical curiosity (cited to [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]]).

## Positions and claims

- The essay's verdict: "Pink was right that people wanted to work for themselves. He was wrong about when they could afford to" — argued in [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]].

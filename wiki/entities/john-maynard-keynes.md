# John Maynard Keynes

> The economist, cited in this wiki as a failed prophet of the shortened workweek.

Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]].

## Key facts

- In 1930, predicted a fifteen-hour workweek by 2030 (cited to [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]]).
- Cited by the essay as a prediction that "is clearly not happening" (cited to [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]]).

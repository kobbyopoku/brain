# Augen Pro

> Cataloged in Refero with an *"Architectural Blueprint on White Marble"* light-mode philosophy (a Stripe / Column sibling phrase) — meticulously placed monochrome elements against a pristine bright background, single vivid blue accent reserved for interactive elements.

## Profile

This page is a **stub**. Augen Pro appears in the wiki only as a Refero-catalog DESIGN.md ingest. No primary source about the brand, its category, or its target market is yet ingested. *Augen* is German for "eyes" — the brand may be in eyewear, eye-care, or vision-tech, but this is unconfirmed.

## Design system summary

- **Theme**: light — high-contrast monochrome (near-black text on near-white surfaces) + single vivid blue accent.
- **Philosophy**: *"understated precision and future-forward serenity … intellectual rigor."* (Same "blueprint on marble" phrase as Stripe and Column — third Refero brand to use it.)
- **Typeface**: **PP Neue Montreal** (single typeface — display, body, UI, buttons, links).
- **Type sizes**: 10 / 12 / 13 / 14 / 16 / 18 / 27 px.
- **Weights**: 350, 400 — extremely light. The 350 weight is unusual; appears in only a handful of brands in the catalog.
- **Mood**: controlled innovation; minimalist; technical elegance.

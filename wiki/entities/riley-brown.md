# Riley Brown

> AI content creator cited in [[wiki/sources/exploraX_-5-solo-ai-business-models]] as the receipt for the **AI Influencer** path.

## Profile

Riley Brown is an AI content creator presented in [[wiki/sources/exploraX_-5-solo-ai-business-models|m0h's guide]] as the case study for the AI Influencer business model. Per that source, he was the first person to post about ChatGPT on TikTok on its launch day — a video that did 20M views — and grew from 0 to 200k followers in two weeks, now sitting at 1.5M+ followers across platforms. He co-founded vibecode.dev.

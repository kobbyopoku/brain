# WhatsApp

## Key facts

- Acquired by Facebook for $19 billion in February 2014 (cited to [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]]).
- Had fifty-five employees at acquisition (cited to [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]]).

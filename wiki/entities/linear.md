From [[wiki/sources/linear-design-md]]: Catalog reference dark-mode system. Pitch Black `#08090a` ground, Neon Lime `#e4f222` as the single bright action color. Twelve-step neutral scale. Inter Variable with non-standard weights 510/590 + Berkeley Mono code. 6px universal radius. Nine shadow tokens — most in the catalog. Three semantic colors named.

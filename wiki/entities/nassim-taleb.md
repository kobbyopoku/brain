This page is a **stub**. Nassim Taleb appears in this wiki only via [[wiki/sources/retrochainer-claude-700k-musk-tweets]], which names him as a philosopher and trader and credits him with formalizing the Barbell Strategy. No further biographical context is sourced here.

## Key facts

- **Described as**: a philosopher and trader ([[wiki/sources/retrochainer-claude-700k-musk-tweets]]).
- **Barbell Strategy**: credited with formalizing it "long before Aniki applied it on Polymarket" ([[wiki/sources/retrochainer-claude-700k-musk-tweets]]).
- **Antifragility**: associated with the concept; the source contrasts "stability plus antifragility" against moderate-risk middle bets ([[wiki/sources/retrochainer-claude-700k-musk-tweets]]).

## Positions and claims

- **The Barbell Strategy** — concentrate exposure at the two extremes (maximal safety on one end, high-convexity bets on the other) rather than in a moderate-risk middle. Attributed to Taleb and applied by the RetroChainer case study; see [[wiki/sources/retrochainer-claude-700k-musk-tweets]].

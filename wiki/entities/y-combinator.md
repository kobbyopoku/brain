# Y Combinator

> Startup accelerator, used in this wiki as a reference point for predictions about solo-founder share and team sizes in the AI era.

Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]].

## Key facts

- **Solo-founder prediction**: the essay predicts at least one YC batch admits more than one third solo founders, versus roughly 25% currently (per [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]]).
- **Demo-Day team size prediction**: the essay predicts the median YC company at Demo Day has fewer than 3 employees, versus 4-7 currently (per [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]]).

# OCaml

> Functional programming language whose ecosystem is dominated by Jane Street's open-source footprint.

Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].

## Key facts

- **Largest commercial user**: Jane Street is the largest commercial user of OCaml in the world and "wrote half of the OCaml ecosystem" (cited to [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]]).
- **Jane Street OCaml libraries**: `core`, `async`, and `hardcaml` are OCaml libraries (cited to [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]]).

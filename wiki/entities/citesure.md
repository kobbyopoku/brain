# CiteSure

> Citation/EdTech B2C web app built by [[wiki/entities/mattgittleson|Matt Gittleson]], marketed with organic short-form video, and sold to [[wiki/entities/jenni-ai|Jenni AI]] for $375,000 in roughly six months.

## Profile

CiteSure is a citation-focused B2C web app — deliberately a web app rather than a native/App Store app, avoiding Xcode, Apple's 30% cut, and App Store review. Built by non-technical solo founder [[wiki/entities/mattgittleson|Matt Gittleson]], it peaked at $132K ARR before being acquired by [[wiki/entities/jenni-ai|Jenni AI]] for a total of $375,000, roughly six months after launch. The product ran at a net profit margin over 80% with total marketing spend under $100, relying on organic short-form video for distribution. Under Jenni AI's ownership it continued growing despite no new videos being posted for almost a year.

## Key facts

- **Form factor**: web app, not native/App Store app — no Xcode, no Apple 30% cut, no App Store review (per [[wiki/sources/mattgittleson-vibecoded-b2c-375k-exit]]).
- **Peak ARR (pre-acquisition)**: ~$132K (per [[wiki/sources/mattgittleson-vibecoded-b2c-375k-exit]]).
- **ARR at acquisition**: ~£75K / $100K, after previously peaking at $132K (per [[wiki/sources/mattgittleson-vibecoded-b2c-375k-exit]]).
- **Exit value**: $375,000 total, roughly six months after launch (per [[wiki/sources/mattgittleson-vibecoded-b2c-375k-exit]]).
- **Economics**: net profit margin over 80% with total marketing spend under $100 (per [[wiki/sources/mattgittleson-vibecoded-b2c-375k-exit]]).
- **Acquisition timing**: buyer DM arrived when the app was less than 2 months old (per [[wiki/sources/mattgittleson-vibecoded-b2c-375k-exit]]).
- **Post-acquisition growth**: grew to ~£135K / $180K ARR (~80% growth) with no new videos posted in almost a year (per [[wiki/sources/mattgittleson-vibecoded-b2c-375k-exit]]).
- **Acquirer**: [[wiki/entities/jenni-ai|Jenni AI]] (per [[wiki/sources/mattgittleson-vibecoded-b2c-375k-exit]]).

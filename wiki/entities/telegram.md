# Telegram

> Messaging app used across several ingested AI-agent workflows as a phone-side delivery and approval channel.

Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/doublenickk-personal-x-agent-algorithm]], [[wiki/sources/cyrilxbt-obsidian-dashboard-everything-today]], and [[wiki/sources/saboo-shubham-ultimate-guide-to-goal]].

## Key facts

- In [[wiki/sources/doublenickk-personal-x-agent-algorithm]]'s Blueprint 02, a Telegram bot delivers post drafts to the user's phone for one-tap approval or rejection; setting up a Telegram bot is listed as a requirement/cost of Blueprint 02.
- In [[wiki/sources/cyrilxbt-obsidian-dashboard-everything-today]], at 6:00 AM the user's phone receives a Telegram notification that the morning briefing is ready in the daily note.
- In [[wiki/sources/saboo-shubham-ultimate-guide-to-goal]], Shubham Saboo messages his Hermes orchestrator over Telegram from his phone to kick off goals.

## Positions and claims

- Recurs as the human-in-the-loop / notification surface for phone-based agent workflows — approval gates, briefing alerts, and goal kickoff — across multiple independent sources.

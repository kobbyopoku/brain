## Design system summary

From [[wiki/sources/superhuman-design-md]]: Single proprietary Super Sans VF typeface (weights 460/500/540/600/700). Aubergine `#421d24` reserved for announcement banner + footer only. Iris `#714cb6` for ghost button outlines and links — never as filled button background. Parchment Canvas `#f2f0eb` ground (never pure white). 64px display at line-height 0.96 — letters effectively touch.

# Grok

> xAI's transformer model; per [[wiki/sources/doublenickk-personal-x-agent-algorithm]], the basis of the For You feed's Phoenix ML ranking model.

Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/doublenickk-personal-x-agent-algorithm]].

## Key facts

- Per the source, the algorithm's Phoenix ML ranking model is a Grok-based transformer (cited to [[wiki/sources/doublenickk-personal-x-agent-algorithm]]).
- The source claims the Grok transformer has eliminated every hand-engineered feature and learns relevance entirely from a user's engagement history (likes / replies / shares / dwell).

## Design system summary

From [[wiki/sources/antimetal-design-md]]: Two-mode: dark navy hero + near-white product surface, bridged by Chartreuse Pulse `#d0f100` for primary CTAs only. Pill buttons (`9999px`) but inputs at `0px` as deliberate signature. Blue-tinted shadows. abcdFont UI + ivarTextFont display with OpenType `ss04, ss06, ss09, ss10, ss11`.

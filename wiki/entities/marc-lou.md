# Marc Lou

> Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/exploraX_-5-solo-ai-business-models]] as a receipt for the "AI App / Micro-SaaS" solo-AI-business path; builder behind several micro-SaaS products and the TrustMRR acquisition marketplace.

## Key facts

- **2021**: broke, living in an underground apartment in South Korea. *(See [[wiki/sources/exploraX_-5-solo-ai-business-models]].)*
- **2025**: did over $1M in a single year from a micro-SaaS portfolio. *(See [[wiki/sources/exploraX_-5-solo-ai-business-models]].)*
- **Portfolio**: ShipFast (~$20k/mo), CodeFast (~$20k/mo), DataFast ($15.8k MRR), TrustMRR (~$35k/mo). *(See [[wiki/sources/exploraX_-5-solo-ai-business-models]].)*
- **Shipped 35 startups total** with a ~5% hit rate. *(See [[wiki/sources/exploraX_-5-solo-ai-business-models]].)*
- **Runs** newsletter.marclou.com. *(See [[wiki/sources/exploraX_-5-solo-ai-business-models]].)*
- **Builder behind TrustMRR**, the acquisition marketplace cited for disrupting TTM pricing. *(See [[wiki/sources/mattgittleson-vibecoded-b2c-375k-exit]].)*

## Positions and claims

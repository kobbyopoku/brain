# Two Sigma

> Quantitative hedge fund featured in the catalog of open-source repos from hedge funds and quant firms.

## Profile

Two Sigma is a quantitative hedge fund with approximately $70B AUM (source-reported). It contributes four catalogued open-source repositories — flint, beakerx, marbles, and cook — to the survey of quant-firm open source compiled in [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].

## Key facts

- **AUM**: ~$70B (source-reported) — per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].
- **Open-source repos catalogued**: flint, beakerx, marbles, cook — per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].
- **Hiring**: described as only hiring PhDs from MIT, Stanford, and CMU — per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].

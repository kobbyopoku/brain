# Jane Street

> Proprietary trading firm and the largest commercial user of OCaml, featured in the catalog of open-source repos from hedge funds and quant firms.

## Profile

Jane Street is a proprietary trading firm that reported approximately $39.6B trading revenue in 2025 and $16.1B in Q1 2026 (source-reported). It is the largest commercial user of OCaml in the world and is credited with writing "half of the OCaml ecosystem." It contributes four OCaml repositories — core, magic-trace, async, and hardcaml — to the survey compiled in [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]], where its open-source publishing is framed as a recruiting strategy.

## Key facts

- **Trading revenue**: ~$39.6B in 2025; $16.1B in Q1 2026 (source-reported) — per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].
- **OCaml**: largest commercial user of OCaml in the world; wrote "half of the OCaml ecosystem" — per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].
- **Codebase**: 30 million lines of code, almost 1 million public on GitHub — per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].
- **Equity volume**: moved $2 trillion in equity volume per month in 2024 — per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].
- **Open-source repos catalogued**: core, magic-trace, async, hardcaml — per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].

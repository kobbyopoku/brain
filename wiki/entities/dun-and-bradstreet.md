# Dun & Bradstreet

> Global business-data and analytics company best known for the [[wiki/entities/d-u-n-s-number|D-U-N-S® Number]], a unique 9-digit business identifier used for verification and due diligence worldwide.

## Profile

Dun & Bradstreet (D&B) is a commercial business-data and analytics provider whose flagship asset is the D-U-N-S® Number — a unique identifier assigned to individual business entities. It appears in this wiki via an outreach email from its **South Asia, Middle East & Africa (SAMEA)** region offering to issue a D-U-N-S Number alongside paid "value-added services." Its products are positioned for financial institutions, multinational corporations, and partners performing verification and due diligence.

## Key facts

- **Flagship product**: the [[wiki/entities/d-u-n-s-number|D-U-N-S® Number]], a unique 9-digit business identifier. (per [[wiki/sources/dnb-duns-number-outreach]])
- **Database scale (vendor-reported, uncited)**: claims coverage of "520+ million businesses worldwide." (per [[wiki/sources/dnb-duns-number-outreach]] — not independently verified.)
- **Adoption claim (vendor-reported, uncited)**: states "90% of Fortune 500 companies rely on it." (per [[wiki/sources/dnb-duns-number-outreach]] — not independently verified.)
- **Regional presence**: a South Asia, Middle East & Africa (SAMEA) division, from which the outreach originated. (per [[wiki/sources/dnb-duns-number-outreach]])

## Positions and claims

- Markets the D-U-N-S Number as the basis of "global identity and credibility" for businesses, widely used for verification and due diligence — argued in [[wiki/sources/dnb-duns-number-outreach]].
- Offers standard D-U-N-S issuance free of charge (~30 working days) while promoting additional paid "value-added services."

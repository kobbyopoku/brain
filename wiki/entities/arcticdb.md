# ArcticDB

> Serverless time-series database open-sourced by Man Group, catalogued here as the one Business Source License repo among hedge-fund open-source projects.

Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].

## Key facts

- **Origin**: Open-sourced by Man Group; ~2.3k GitHub stars (source-reported) — per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].
- **Function**: Stores billions of rows of time-series data in S3 with no server; pandas in, pandas out — per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].
- **Adoption**: Licensed by Bloomberg from Man Group — per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].
- **License**: Uses the Business Source License (BSL): free for non-production use, paid for commercial deployment — per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].

# Google

> Big-tech company positioned across this wiki as a frontier-AI provider — Gemini, an "AI operating layer for the internet," agent-protocol authorship, and AI education resources.

## Profile

Google appears across multiple wiki sources as a frontier-AI player rather than a single-product subject. At Google I/O 2026 it is framed as building an "AI operating layer for the internet" with Gemini at the center, integrating AI across Search, Workspace, Android, YouTube, Shopping, Chrome, Cloud, XR, and developer tools. It is a co-author of the ERC-8004 agent-identity standard and the author of the A2A agent-to-agent protocol and the Google ADK orchestration framework. It is also a named big-tech AI-engineer employer, a model provider behind enterprise LLM proxies, and a provider of AI education resources (the AI Professional Certificate and grow.google/ai).

## Key facts

- **I/O 2026 framing**: At Google I/O 2026, Google is described as "no longer building apps" but "an AI operating layer for the internet" (cited to [[wiki/sources/shabnam-google-2026-roadmap-keynote]]).
- **Search upgrade**: Google described the Search update as "the biggest Search upgrade in 25 years" (per cited Times of India coverage) (cited to [[wiki/sources/shabnam-google-2026-roadmap-keynote]]).
- **AI integration breadth**: Announced AI integration across Search, Workspace, Android, YouTube, Shopping, Chrome, Cloud, XR, developer tools, and productivity workflows (cited to [[wiki/sources/shabnam-google-2026-roadmap-keynote]]).
- **AI-engineer pay**: Named (with Amazon and Meta) as paying AI engineers up to $350k/year in 2026 (cited to [[wiki/sources/exploraX_-5-solo-ai-business-models]]).
- **Model provider**: One of the model providers (with OpenAI and Anthropic) behind Shopify's LLM proxy (cited to [[wiki/sources/zodchiii-shopify-23000-engineers-claude-code-setup]]).
- **Agent standards**: Among the ERC-8004 standard authors; authors the A2A agent-to-agent protocol; Google ADK (Agent Development Kit) is named among orchestration frameworks (cited to [[wiki/sources/awrigh01-technical-stack-autonomous-agents]]).
- **FDE demand**: Named (with Anthropic and OpenAI) as an AI company looking for FDEs (cited to [[wiki/sources/vasuman-forward-deployed-engineering-101]]).
- **AI education**: Google's AI Professional Certificate is named a top Phase-1 starting point (workflows/prompting/use-cases without front-loading math); free Google AI resources at grow.google/ai (cited to [[wiki/sources/Shruti_0810-zero-to-ai-engineer-roadmap]]).

## Positions and claims

- Frames its goal as becoming "the operating system for the AI era," not winning the chatbot war (cited to [[wiki/sources/shabnam-google-2026-roadmap-keynote]]).
- Coinbase and Google co-authoring an Ethereum ERC for agent identity is cited as the convergence story between crypto-native and enterprise-native camps (cited to [[wiki/sources/awrigh01-technical-stack-autonomous-agents]]).

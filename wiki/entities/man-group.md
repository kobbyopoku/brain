# Man Group

> Publicly listed hedge fund featured in the catalog of open-source repos from hedge funds and quant firms.

## Profile

Man Group is described as the largest publicly listed hedge fund in the world, with approximately $228.7B AUM (source-reported). Its systematic trading unit is AHL. It contributes four catalogued open-source repositories — ArcticDB, dtale, notebooker, and PyBloqs — to the survey compiled in [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].

## Key facts

- **AUM**: ~$228.7B (source-reported); described as the largest publicly listed hedge fund in the world — per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].
- **Open-source repos catalogued**: ArcticDB, dtale, notebooker, PyBloqs — per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].
- **Systematic trading unit**: AHL — per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].
- **Licensing**: Bloomberg licensed ArcticDB from Man Group — per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].

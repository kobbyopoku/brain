## Positions and claims

- **MCP and Skills are not the same thing.** Conflating them is *"one of the most common mistakes when people start building AI agents seriously."*
- **The biggest shift in AI agents (2022-26) hasn't been about making models smarter — it's been about making the environment around them smarter.** Affordances, tools, harnesses, memory.
- **Memory-based RL beats weight-based RL for many agent contexts.** Memento-style architectures.

# Apple App Store

> Apple's mobile app distribution surface. Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/rork-scale-mobile-app-50k]] as the launch surface for AI-built apps and the source of the "Apple boost."

## Profile

This page is a **stub**. The Apple App Store appears only via [[wiki/sources/rork-scale-mobile-app-50k]], where it is the distribution surface for AI-built mobile apps. The guide names the "Apple boost" as a launch-day distribution mechanism but cautions that it is rarely enough on its own.

## Key facts

- **"Apple boost"**: described as "Apple's way of pushing your app to new audiences when you first launch it" ([[wiki/sources/rork-scale-mobile-app-50k]]).
- **Boost limitation**: most apps "won't get any traction on launch day even with the Apple boost" ([[wiki/sources/rork-scale-mobile-app-50k]]).

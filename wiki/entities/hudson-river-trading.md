# Hudson River Trading

> High-frequency trading firm featured in the catalog of open-source repos from hedge funds and quant firms.

## Profile

Hudson River Trading is a high-frequency trading firm with approximately $20B trading capital (source-reported). It executes up to 5% of all U.S. equity trades every day and reported $6.4B trading revenue in Q1 2026, a 135% YoY jump. Its systems are written in C++, FPGA, and SystemVerilog, and it contributes three catalogued open-source repositories — corral, slang-server, and heracles-ql — per [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].

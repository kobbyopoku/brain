# Google Workspace

> Google's productivity suite: **Gmail + Calendar + Drive + Docs + Sheets in one connector**. Per Ole Lehmann: *"Absolutely essential. If your agent can't read your inbox or write to your docs, it can't really work for you, so plug this in before anything else."* Distinct from the individual [[wiki/entities/gmail]] and [[wiki/entities/google-drive]] entities — Google Workspace is the *unified connector* covering all five services.

# Sajjaad Khader

> AI educator cited in [[wiki/sources/exploraX_-5-solo-ai-business-models]] as the receipt for the **AI Engineer** path; author of a 5-layer AI-engineer roadmap.

## Profile

Sajjaad Khader is an AI engineer and educator presented in [[wiki/sources/exploraX_-5-solo-ai-business-models|m0h's guide]] as the case study for the AI Engineer business model. Per that source, he earned a master's in AI + computer science from Georgia Tech at age 20, worked at Amazon, and runs a 297k-subscriber YouTube channel teaching the AI-engineer roadmap while selling paid courses. He authored a 5-layer AI-engineer pyramid spanning software foundation, basic AI integration, intelligent systems, scale, and strategic operations.

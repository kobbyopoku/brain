# OpenVINO

> Intel's optimized inference story for its CPUs, GPUs, and NPUs.

## Profile

OpenVINO is Intel's inference toolkit, with OpenVINO GenAI and the OpenVINO Model Server providing the Intel-optimized path for running large language models. It targets Intel hardware — Xeon CPUs, Arc GPUs, Core Ultra, and NPUs — and offers OpenAI-compatible serving with modern serving features.

## Key facts

- **Hardware**: OpenVINO GenAI is the Intel-optimized story for Xeon CPUs, Arc GPUs, Core Ultra, and NPUs (cited to [[wiki/sources/theahmadosman-inference-engines-local-ai-hardware]]).
- **Serving**: offers OpenAI-compatible serving with continuous batching and paged attention (cited to [[wiki/sources/theahmadosman-inference-engines-local-ai-hardware]]).
- **Best fit**: Intel hardware (cited to [[wiki/sources/theahmadosman-inference-engines-local-ai-hardware]]).

# Contractbook

> Contract management / digital contracting platform. Cataloged in Refero with a *"playful professionalism, high-contrast clarity"* light-mode philosophy — bold primary colors (vibrant yellow + strong blue) on a clean spacious background.

## Profile

This page is a **stub**. Contractbook appears in the wiki only as a Refero-catalog DESIGN.md ingest. No primary source about the company or its market is yet ingested.

## Design system summary

- **Theme**: light — clean spacious background; bold primary colors; visual hierarchy through color blocking + generous whitespace, not elevation.
- **Philosophy**: *"playful, confident aesthetic"* — robust direct typography; geometric soft shapes throughout.
- **Typeface**: **Abcwhyte** (single typeface — clean, slightly humanist sans-serif; headings to body to UI).
- **Type sizes**: 11 / 12 / 14 / 16 / 25 / 28 / 32 / 40 / 48 px.
- **Weights**: 400, 700.
- **Brand signature**: vibrant yellow for primary actions; strong blue for statements.

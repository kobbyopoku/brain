# Flint

> Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].

Open-source library from Two Sigma for time-series joins on Apache Spark.

## Key facts

- **Maintainer**: open-sourced by Two Sigma (cited to [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]]).
- **Stars**: ~1k (source-reported).
- **Function**: provides time-series joins on top of Apache Spark with temporal tolerance (e.g. nearest-quote-for-every-trade) (cited to [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]]).

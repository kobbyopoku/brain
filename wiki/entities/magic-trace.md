# magic-trace

> Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]].

Open-source high-resolution process tracer from Jane Street; its most-starred repo.

## Key facts

- **Maintainer**: open-sourced by Jane Street (cited to [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]]).
- **Stars**: ~5.3k — the most-starred Jane Street repo (source-reported).
- **Function**: high-resolution process tracer powered by Intel Processor Trace; shows every CPU instruction a process executed (cited to [[wiki/sources/zostaff-22-hedge-fund-quant-open-source-repos]]).

# AMD

> Semiconductor company and GPU vendor; appears in this wiki as a hardware target for local-AI inference engines via ROCm/HIP. See [[wiki/sources/theahmadosman-inference-engines-local-ai-hardware]].

## Key facts

- **Engine support**: AMD support via HIP in [[wiki/entities/llama-cpp|llama.cpp]] and ROCm in vLLM/[[wiki/entities/sglang|SGLang]], cited in [[wiki/sources/theahmadosman-inference-engines-local-ai-hardware]].
- **Datacenter GPUs**: MI300, MI325, MI350, MI355, cited in [[wiki/sources/theahmadosman-inference-engines-local-ai-hardware]].

## Positions and claims

- **NVIDIA benchmarks do not transfer cleanly to AMD** — the source warns not to assume NVIDIA benchmark results carry over to AMD hardware. See [[wiki/sources/theahmadosman-inference-engines-local-ai-hardware]].

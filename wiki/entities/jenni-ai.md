# Jenni AI

> AI writing/research company that acquired [[wiki/entities/citesure|CiteSure]] and that [[wiki/entities/mattgittleson|Matt Gittleson]] joined after his exit.

## Profile

Jenni AI is the company that acquired [[wiki/entities/citesure|CiteSure]] from [[wiki/entities/mattgittleson|Matt Gittleson]]. The relationship began when Jenni AI reached out to one of Gittleson's content accounts for a content partnership, believing it to be a real creator account; that contact turned into the acquisition. Gittleson describes Jenni AI as run by "one of the greatest founders of our generation" and reports the company is at $1M MRR. After the exit he moved to Korea to join Jenni AI.

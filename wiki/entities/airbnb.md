# Airbnb

> YC unicorn (~$80B market cap) and short-stay rental marketplace. Cited in two distinct ways in this wiki: as a [[landing-page-patterns]] exemplar in [[wiki/sources/clear_graphics-yc-unicorn-landing-pages]], and as a Refero-catalog DESIGN.md brand in [[wiki/sources/airbnb-design-md]] — second wiki entity (after [[wiki/entities/stripe|Stripe]] and [[wiki/entities/anthropic|Anthropic]]) cited at both layers.

## Profile

Airbnb's landing page is a reference example for several [[landing-page-patterns|landing-page patterns]]: category-defining headline (*"belong anywhere"*), two-sided marketplace audience-split (guests / hosts tabs), and minimal navigation (3 items). Airbnb's design system, captured by Refero, is a *"vacation photos pinned to a white corkboard"* aesthetic — bright photography in rounded frames against near-white surfaces, with a single coral pin (`#ff385c`) holding everything together.

## Design system summary

- **Theme**: light — `#f7f7f7` canvas with `#ffffff` card surfaces; barely-there depth; photography does the visual heavy lifting.
- **Philosophy**: *"vacation photos pinned to a white corkboard — bright photography contained in rounded frames against a near-white surface, with a single coral pin holding everything together."*
- **Typeface**: **Airbnb Cereal VF** (single custom variable font for entire UI — weights 400/500/600/700).
- **Type sizes**: 8 / 11 / 12 / 13 / 14 / 16 / 20 / 21 / 22 / 28 px. Compact scale.
- **Brand signature**: **Coral red `#ff385c`** appears exclusively on brand touchpoints (logo, active states, search button) — feels like a signature, not a system color.
- **Shape signature**: card images bleed edge-to-edge with **20px rounded corners** — the only generous radius in an otherwise compact information-dense layout. Negative letter-spacing -0.02em pulls large numerals together. The `salt` font feature distinguishes it from system sans-serifs.

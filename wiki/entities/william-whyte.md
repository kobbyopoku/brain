# William Whyte

> Author. Appears in this wiki via [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]], cited as the originator of the term "the Organization Man".

## Key facts

- Coined the term "the Organization Man" — per [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]].
- Described the archetype as one who had "left home, spiritually as well as physically, to take the vows of organization life" — per [[wiki/sources/kushwah-aaryan-future-of-work]].

# Google Maps

> Google's mapping product, surfaced in the wiki specifically as a **prospect-discovery surface for AI-services builders**. [[wiki/sources/exploraX_-google-maps-leadgen|m0h's playbook]] treats Google Maps as a free, pre-qualified database of small businesses with observable gaps (low review counts, no website, no review-responses, missing basics) — turning it into a lead-gen funnel.

## Key facts

- **Prospect-discovery channel**: listed (alongside LinkedIn and local business directories) as a channel to find businesses in a chosen niche for direct outreach — cited to [[wiki/sources/eng_khairallah1-real-money-ai-automations]].
- **Free lead source for website-selling**: framed as the prospect list for the website-selling model — every business with a bad or no website is on it; filter by industry and build a list — cited to [[wiki/sources/exploraX_-5-solo-ai-business-models]].

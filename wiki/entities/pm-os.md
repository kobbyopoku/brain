# PM OS

> [[wiki/entities/nurijanian|nurijanian]]'s product: an operating layer for product management that encodes agent-ready PM judgment into reusable workflows.

## Profile

PM OS is [[wiki/entities/nurijanian|nurijanian]]'s product that gives product work an operating layer — memory, context, workflows, assumptions, research, decisions, measurement, and review. Its purpose is to encode the tacit judgment of product managers (acceptance criteria, edge cases, validation evidence, stop conditions) into reusable workflows, so that each piece of work starts with a sharper definition of done. It ships packaged with external skills, and a v2 was announced as shipping within days of the 2026-05-10 post that introduced it.

- **Builder**: [[wiki/entities/nurijanian|nurijanian]] — [[wiki/sources/nurijanian-goal-for-product-managers]].
- **What it is**: an operating layer for product work spanning memory, context, workflows, assumptions, research, decisions, measurement, and review — [[wiki/sources/nurijanian-goal-for-product-managers]].

- **v2**: announced as shipping "in a few days" (relative to the 2026-05-10 post) with improved memory, agents, and skill workflows — [[wiki/sources/nurijanian-goal-for-product-managers]].

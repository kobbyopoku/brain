The entire playbook for deploying AI agents inside a company fits in 3 phases: Audit → Evals → Deployment Audit: - Find the high-volume workflows where the improvement is large enough to matter. Evals: - Turn non-determinism into evidence. - 50 runs, 41 pass. - Of the 9 that missed: 5 missing data, 4 wrong record pulled. - Feed that back in. Deployment: - Build on top of the stack they already run (NetSuite, Salesforce, SAP.) - Shadow mode → more autonomy → production. Think: - Audit = where to build - Evals = proof it's safe to act on - Deployment = walk them through the journey Improve one system and the next one becomes obvious.

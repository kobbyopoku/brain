[Vanessa](https://x.com/hustlepotato)[@hustlepotato](https://x.com/hustlepotato)

Every health app treats you as an average person having an average day. But we are all built differently in life. Different histories, life experiences, personality. The same HRV reading could mean two different things in two different bodies, and a third thing depending on where you are in your cycle. An app that doesn't know who you are is just reading a number out loud. That personalization is a lie. What it takes instead: baseline you (trait) → today you (cycle phase) → right-now you (autonomic signal). Stop reading the number. Start reading the person.

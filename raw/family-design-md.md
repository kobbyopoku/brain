## Design Philosophy

"Warm off-white canvas (#fbfaf9) littered with expressive flat-illustrated characters in vivid primary colors." The illustration style—"wobbly blob creatures with stick legs and expressive faces"—functions as the primary identity system. Cards employ "inset warm-stone border (1px shadow)" rather than drop shadows, maintaining flat tactility.

**Key Rules:**
- Never use pure #ffffff as page background
- Apply tight negative letter-spacing to large text
- Restrict Family typeface to display/heading sizes only
- Use Ember Orange exclusively for text-link CTAs
- Position illustration characters asymmetrically, overlapping headline text for depth
- Avoid drop shadows on content cards
